I'm a software engineer with a systems-level mindset — someone who thinks carefully about architecture before writing a single line of code. My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering, infrastructure, and business operations.

Having served as CTO at Netpipo Ltd, I understand what it means to make high-stakes technical decisions that affect real users. My experience spans backend development, API integrations with financial systems (UrubutoPay, QuickBooks, IremboPay), process automation, and DevOps. Whether working independently or with distributed teams, I bring rigour and clarity to every engagement.

Based in Kigali, Rwanda, I'm fully equipped for remote collaboration and actively seeking impactful global projects and long-term remote roles.
