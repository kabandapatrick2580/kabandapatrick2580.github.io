I'm a software engineer with a systems-level mindset — someone who thinks carefully about architecture before writing a single line of code. My work sits at the intersection of backend engineering, infrastructure, and business operations.

Having served as CTO at Netpipo Ltd, I understand what it means to make high-stakes technical decisions that affect real users. I've built production systems end-to-end — from database schema to deployment pipelines — and I take pride in shipping software that is reliable, maintainable, and secure.
